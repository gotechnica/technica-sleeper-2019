Technica is looking for students from around the country to work alongside our 2019 organizing team!

Help promote our message of inclusion in STEM and represent Technica at your school!

Ambassadors Should...

Be a point of contact for marketing and travel


Provide info-booths on their campus


Coordinate buses from you to us

Merit-based Perks!

Individual features on our social media page!


Custom Technica merchandise!


Sponsored prizes!

Apply today!

Application Deadline: July 8th @ 11:59PM

FAQ

What does merit-based mean?

If selected as a Campus Ambassador, we’ll be sure to explain to you further the details of our merit-based system. All you need to know is, the more effort you put into your role and outreach as an Ambassador, the more perks you’ll receive!

When is Technica 2019?

The tentative date for Technica 2019 is November 9th - 10th.

I don’t identify as a woman or non-binary person, can I still apply?

Yes, of course! All genders and allies are welcome to be an Ambassador!

I’ve never been to Technica, can I still apply?

Yes, of course! We always welcome first-time attendees to Technica and this is no exception!

I’m in high school, can I still apply?

Yes! Any full-time student, as of November 2019, from 9th grade onwards is eligible to apply!

How much of a time commitment will this role require?

If you’re selected as a Campus Ambassador, all we require is that you attend Technica 2019 (stay tuned for the exact date)! This a merit-based program, so you’ll get out of it as much as you put in!

When will I hear back if I was selected as a Campus Ambassador?

Our branding team will be getting back to you between the end of July and beginning of August!

Still have questions? Reach us at branding@gotechnica.org!
